FIRE SPRINKLER ENGINES
What we can offer You ?

We offer Professional Engine Assistance in so many ways! Full Engine Re machining, Rebuilding, Site Removals and fitment! Site Flow testing and engine power optimization. This service is carried out by our highly skilled Mobile team with specialist lifting equipment. It’s returned to our works, stripped,cleaned and photographed. Full technical report written, identifying the cause of the failure! A quote given to reinstate the engine to the BS standard set down. Work can proceed without delay once approval is given.

How long does it take? If we have one on the shelf, we can replace a set in a Day!


For Areas of Very High Risk it is possible to complete the whole operation in days!
